The books we read stay with us for life and are important stepping stones in our development but it's easy to forget them and forget the wisdom they pass on to us.

Booklogr, in the same vein as GoodReads, is a way to keep track of everything you have read, are currently reading and would like to read someday.

Built using Flutter and available on both iOS and Android.
